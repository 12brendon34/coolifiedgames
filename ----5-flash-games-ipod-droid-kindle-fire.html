First of all, only some online Flash games will work on mobile devices.  It depends on the game's controls.  If you need to use the keyboard (like the arrow keys) to control the game, then that game won't work.

Droid:

To play this game on Droids (and others), go to your market place and get the Flash app.  (Just search the market place for "flash" and it will come up.)  With that installed, you should be able to use your device's default browser with no problem.  Hopefully, you'll want to put a bookmark to CoolifiedGames.com Mobile on your home screen!

Kindle Fire:

Your Kindle Fire should already be set up to play Flash games.  If it isn't, download Flash from the app store.  Then, you'll need to make sure that Flash is enabled in your browser.  Go to Web on your Kindle Fire.

Click on the menu icon and click on settings:

Scroll down to find Flash and open the options menu:

Select either "always on" or "on demand."  The second option will eat up less battery life.

iPad, iPad 2 and iPad 3:

To play this game on an iPad, iPad 2 and iPad 3, you'll need to go to iTunes and download the Rover Browser.  It's a free app that was specially designed for educational websites. 

IMPORTANT NOTE:  Use two fingers to scroll down a webpage in Rover!

Here's what you'll need to do the first time you come to CoolifiedGames.com Mobile (once you have the Rover browser installed):

Copy the URL for our mobile site from here.  To do this, hold your finger down on it and, once it highlights, swipe your finger across to grab the whole thing, select "copy" from the menu that comes up.

coolifiedgames.com

Open the Rover Browser:

Hold your finger down in the field where you type website URLs...  Click on "paste" when it comes up.  Then, hit "GO" on the keyboard!

Now, you'll want to bookmark CoolifiedGames.com, so you can get to us a lot faster next time.  Click on the little book and a menu will open.  Click on "Add Bookmark"

Our name will get put in.  Hit the "save" button:

Unfortunately, it is not possible to set up Rover as the default browser on your device, so you won't be able to get to use by just doing a regular bookmark on your home screen.

The next time you want to come to Coolmath-Games.com Mobile...

Open Rover:

Go to bookmarks...  Click on the "Organize" link...

And pick Mobile Cool Math Games the list:

Or you can click on the "More Content" button on the Rover browser and you'll find Coolmath4kids.com in the second row.  Just click on the "math games" icon there.

Sorry, but Flash games won't work on the iPhone or iPod Touch yet.
