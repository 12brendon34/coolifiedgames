If we like your game, we will be happy to purchase a non-exclusive or exclusive license to host it on Coolified Games.

Here are our game requirements:

  • The game must be non-violent.
  • The game must be VERY kid friendly - meaning not even a slightly bad word anywhere.
  • The game must be free of advertising.  (This also means no ad for your own site or company.)  Of course, you can have a "credits" page which can contain your name or your company's name, but not as a URL or link.
  • The game must be free of any and all external links.
  • The game must not contain any sort of stats counter that will report back to you.
  • We will also ask you to disable any "high score" function that your game may have.

If your game meets all of the above requirements, but is a great "thinking" game, we may consider it for Coolmath-Games.com.
