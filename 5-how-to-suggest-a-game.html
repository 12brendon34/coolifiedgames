Hey, have you played a cool game somewhere else and want us to put it on Coolified Games? 

We are always hunting for new cool games, so we would love your help!

The game needs to be non-violent and safe for kids. 

We're look for fun, mindless games for Coolified Games in the categories of "Car / Racing Games," "Shooting Games," "Action / Sports Games," "Adventure Games," and "Arcade Games."  But, if you find a great thinking game, let us know for Coolmath-Games.com!

We can't promise that we can get the game, but, if it's good, we will definitely try our best!

Sometimes, we can't even find a way to contact who made the game.

Sometimes, they won't take their ads and links off for us (then, we can't use it.)

Sometimes, they've already promised to let another site be the only site that can have it and can't break their promise.

We will try though!

If the game is a sequel (or prequel) to a game we already have, then you don't need to tell me about it because I already know.  If I've been able to get it, I will put it up after the other one has been up for awhile!  8-)
